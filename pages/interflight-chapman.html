InterFlight Chapman

Date/Time: 12/11 • Afternoon

Game Description

Each player on the two-person team tees off, then they switch balls for their second shots. After the second shot, the team selects the better ball and plays alternate shots into the hole.

Handicaps

Take 60% of the lower-handicap player’s handicap and 40% of the higher-handicap player’s handicap. Add those two numbers together, then round down to get the team’s total handicap.
