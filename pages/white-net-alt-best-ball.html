White Net Alternate Best Ball

Date/Time: 12/12 • Morning

Game Description

Each player hits their own tee shot. After the tee shots, you and your teammate switch balls and play your partner’s ball from where it lies. You continue to hit the ball your teammate last struck until the ball is holed. The team records one score for the hole — whichever ball took fewer strokes to get into the hole.

Handicaps

Take 60% of the lower-handicap player’s handicap and 40% of the higher-handicap player’s handicap. Add those two numbers together, then round down to get the team’s total handicap.
