InterFlight Scramble

Date/Time: 12/12 • Afternoon

Game Description

Both players on the team hit a tee shot, choose the best result, and then both play their next shots from that location. This process continues until the ball is holed. Throughout the match you must use at least 7 of each players shots from the tee.

Handicaps

Take 35% of the lower-handicap player’s handicap and 15% of the higher-handicap player’s handicap. Add those two numbers together, then round down to get the team’s total handicap.
