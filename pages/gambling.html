Gambling Summary

There are 16 players, and each one pays $300 to join, making a total pot of $4,800. The money is divided like this:

  • $1,920 is for matches. Each player who wins a match within their foursome earns $40.
  • $1,280 is for Net Skins, Gross Skins, and Closest to the Pin (CTP) prizes. Seperate pots for Blue Flight, White Flight, and Interflight Rounds.
  • $400 goes to the team that wins between Team Ricky and Team Sherman.
  • $400 goes to the team that wins between Team Mike and Team Ruben.
  • $800 goes to the flight (Blue or White) that wins the InterFlight competition.

Games and payouts will be tracked through the Squabbit app.
